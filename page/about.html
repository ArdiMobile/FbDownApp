The Story Behind The Fastest Free Video Downloader

What is Galmee?

Galmee is a powerful, 100% free online tool designed to help you download videos from Facebook and Instagram in stunning HD quality. Whether it's a Facebook Reel, Watch video, or an Instagram Story, Reel, or post — Galmee handles it all with lightning speed.

No registration. No software installation. No hidden fees. Just paste your link and download instantly. Built with love in Ethiopia 🇪🇹 for the world.

💡 Fun Fact: The name "Galmee" comes from the Oromo language, meaning "my journey" or "my path" — reflecting our belief that every download is part of someone's digital journey.

How Galmee Works

Galmee uses advanced video extraction technology to fetch video URLs from Facebook and Instagram. When you paste a link, our system:

  1. Detects the platform (Facebook or Instagram)
  2. Extracts all available video qualities
  3. Presents download options in an easy format
  4. Delivers your video with one click

All processing happens in real-time. We never store your videos or personal information on our servers.

Building free tools for the internet. Passionate about clean code and great user experiences. 🇪🇹

Yasin Gelma is a self-taught full-stack web developer and tech entrepreneur from Ethiopia. With a deep passion for coding and problem-solving, he has dedicated his career to building tools that make the internet more useful and accessible for everyone.

In 2026, Yasin founded Galmee Inc. and launched its flagship product — the Galmee Video Downloader. What started as a simple idea has grown into one of the most reliable Facebook and Instagram video downloading tools, serving thousands of users worldwide.

Yasin specializes in front-end development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and back-end systems (Python, Node.js). He believes in writing clean, efficient code and creating intuitive user interfaces that "just work." Every line of Galmee's code was written by his own hands — no templates, no shortcuts.
