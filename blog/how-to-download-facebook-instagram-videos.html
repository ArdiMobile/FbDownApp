Step 1: Find Your Video

Open Facebook or Instagram on your device. Navigate to the video, reel, story, or post you want to download. Make sure the video is from a public account - private account videos cannot be downloaded due to privacy policies.

For Facebook, you can download: Videos from News Feed, Facebook Watch, Reels, and public group videos.

For Instagram, you can download: Reels, Stories (from public accounts), Feed posts with videos, and IGTV content.

Step 2: Copy the Video Link

This is the most important step. Here's how to copy the link:

On Facebook:

  1. Tap the Share button below the video
  2. Select Copy Link from the menu
  3. The video URL is now copied to your clipboard

On Instagram:

  1. Tap the three dots (•••) in the top-right corner of the post
  2. Select Copy Link from the options
  3. For stories, tap the paper airplane icon and select Copy Link

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Video Not Found?

Make sure the video is from a public account. Private videos cannot be accessed by any downloader tool.

Link Not Detected?

Try manually pasting the link into the search box. Sometimes clipboard permissions need to be enabled.

No Sound in Video?

This is rare with Galmee, but if it happens, try a different quality option. Some formats merge audio differently.
