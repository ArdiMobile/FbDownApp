Downloading Facebook videos in HD quality has never been easier. Whether you want to save a tutorial, a funny video, or important content for offline viewing, our Facebook Video Downloader tool makes it simple and fast.

Why Download Facebook Videos?

There are many reasons why you might want to download videos from Facebook:

  • Offline viewing: Watch videos without an internet connection
  • Save important content: Preserve tutorials, lectures, or memories
  • Share elsewhere: Repost content on other platforms (with permission)
  • Backup: Keep copies of your own uploaded videos

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Find Your Video

Open Facebook and navigate to the video you want to download. It can be a regular video post, a reel, or a video from a page or group.

Step 2: Copy the Video Link

Tap the Share button below the video, then select Copy Link. The video URL is now saved to your clipboard.

Pro Tip: Our downloader automatically detects Facebook links from your clipboard! Just copy and come back to this page.

Step 3: Paste and Download

Return to our downloader page and paste the link into the search box. Our tool will fetch the video and show you all available quality options.

Step 4: Choose Your Quality

Select from available qualities including:

  • 1080p Full HD - Best quality for large screens
  • 720p HD - Great balance of quality and file size
  • 480p SD - Smaller file size, good for mobile
  • 360p & 240p - Fast download, minimal data usage
