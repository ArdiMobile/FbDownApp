1. Use a Fast Internet Connection

The faster your internet, the quicker Galmee processes your video. On 4G/5G, most videos are ready in under 5 seconds.

2. Copy Links Directly from the App

Always use the official Facebook or Instagram app's "Copy Link" feature for the most reliable URLs.

3. Choose the Right Quality

1080p gives the best quality but takes longer. For quick downloads on mobile, 720p or 480p is faster.

4. Download During Off-Peak Hours

Early morning or late night downloads are faster when servers have less traffic.

5. Clear Your Browser Cache

Regularly clearing cache ensures Galmee runs at optimal speed.
