Tips to Download Instagram Videos & Fix Common Errors (2026 Guide)

June 28, 2026 Yasin Gelma 12.8K views 7 min read

Downloading Instagram videos should be simple, but sometimes errors pop up. Whether you're trying to save a Reel, Story, or post, this comprehensive guide will help you fix common Instagram download errors and get your videos successfully every time.

💡 Quick Tip: With Galmee, most Instagram videos download in under 10 seconds. If you hit an error, the solutions below will fix it fast!

Why Instagram Video Downloads Sometimes Fail

Instagram is constantly updating its platform to protect content. This means downloader tools like Galmee must continuously adapt. Here are the most common reasons downloads fail:

  • Private Accounts: Videos from private accounts cannot be accessed
  • Expired Links: Story links expire after 24 hours
  • Rate Limiting: Instagram temporarily blocks too many requests
  • Login Requirements: Some content needs authentication
  • Deleted Content: The video was removed by the user or Instagram
  • Regional Restrictions: Some content is geo-blocked

Error #1: "Video Not Available" or "Content Not Found"

This is the most common error when downloading Instagram videos. It usually means one of these:

Error Message: "Content Not Available"

This video may be private, deleted, or the link has expired.

How to Fix:

  1. Check if the account is public: Go to Instagram and check if you can view the video without logging in. If it requires login, the account is private.
  2. Verify the link: Copy the link again directly from Instagram. Story links expire after 24 hours.
  3. Try a different video: Test with a known public video to confirm Galmee is working correctly.
  4. Check if the video was deleted: Sometimes users delete their posts or stories.

If the account is public and the link is fresh, but you still get this error, wait 5-10 minutes and try again. Instagram may be rate-limiting requests.

Error #2: "Login Required" or "Authentication Needed"

Instagram occasionally requires users to be logged in to view certain content, even if the account is public. This is a security measure by Instagram.

Error Message: "Login Required"

This content requires authentication to view. Private account or age-restricted content.

How to Fix:

  1. Use a public account link: Private accounts always require login - these cannot be downloaded.
  2. Check age restrictions: Some content is marked as sensitive and requires login.
  3. Try again later: Instagram sometimes flags downloader traffic temporarily.

Note: Galmee cannot bypass Instagram's login requirements. This is a platform restriction, not a tool limitation.

Error #3: "Rate Limit Exceeded" or "Too Many Requests"

Instagram limits how many requests can be made in a short period. If you've been downloading many videos quickly, you may hit this limit.

Error Message: "Rate Limit" or "Too Many Requests"

Instagram has temporarily blocked requests from this service. Please wait a few minutes.

How to Fix:

  1. Wait 5-15 minutes: Rate limits are temporary. Take a short break between downloads.
  2. Don't download in rapid succession: Space out your downloads by at least 30 seconds.
  3. Use a different video link: Sometimes only specific content triggers rate limits.
  4. Clear your browser cache: This can help reset rate limit counters.

Error #4: "No Downloadable Formats Found"

This happens when Galmee can access the page but cannot extract a video URL from it. It's usually because Instagram presents the content differently.

How to Fix:

  1. Make sure it's a video post: Carousel posts with mixed photos/videos sometimes confuse extractors.
  2. Try copying from a different place: Copy the link from the post itself, not from a shared message.
  3. For Stories: Make sure the story is still active (not expired).
  4. For Reels: Use the share button on the Reel itself to get the correct URL.

Error #5: "Connection Error" or Timeout

Network issues on your end or Instagram's servers being slow can cause timeouts.

How to Fix:

  1. Check your internet connection: Switch between Wi-Fi and mobile data.
  2. Refresh the page: Sometimes a simple refresh solves connectivity issues.
  3. Try a different browser or device: Isolate whether it's a device-specific issue.

Best Practices for Smooth Instagram Downloads

  • ✅ Use Public Videos: Only public account videos can be downloaded
  • ✅ Copy Fresh Links: Links copied directly from the Instagram app work best
  • ✅ Space Out Downloads: Wait 30-60 seconds between each download
  • ✅ Check Link Format: Links should start with instagram.com/reel/ or instagram.com/p/
  • ✅ Avoid VPNs: Some VPNs trigger Instagram's security measures
  • ✅ Update Your Browser: Older browsers may have compatibility issues
  • ✅ Use Galmee During Off-Peak: Early mornings have less Instagram traffic
